The Best Movies According to Raunak

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Notebook

This is romance story of how a summer love end up becoming perfect love.

Inception

This is one of the best sci-fi movie. The story revolves around the idea of consciousness in dream.

Wolf of the Wall street

Perfect example of how money can change the lifestyle of a person. A story of a broker who used to be broke and end up being financially rich and successful.
